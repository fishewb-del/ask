# Foundation Specification — Project Northgate

## Concrete
All footings shall use 4000 psi concrete with a maximum slump of 4 inches.
The mix shall include Type II portland cement and air entrainment of 5% to 7%
for freeze-thaw resistance. Curing must continue for a minimum of 7 days.

## Reinforcement
Footing reinforcement shall be #5 rebar at 12 inches on center, both ways.
Foundation walls require #4 vertical bars at 16 inches on center with #4
horizontal bars at 18 inches on center. Maintain 3 inches of cover for
concrete cast against earth.

## Excavation and Bearing
Footings shall bear on undisturbed soil with a minimum bearing capacity of
2000 pounds per square foot. The geotechnical engineer must inspect and
approve all bearing surfaces before concrete placement. Frost depth in this
region is 42 inches; bottom of footings shall be no shallower than that.

## Waterproofing
Below-grade foundation walls shall receive a fluid-applied membrane
waterproofing. Install a perimeter drain of 4 inch perforated pipe in washed
gravel, sloped to daylight or a sump.
